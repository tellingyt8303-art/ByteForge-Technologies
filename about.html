The story

Why ByteForge exists

Most businesses need software that actually works in production — not a pitch deck. ByteForge was built around that idea: pick a small number of things (trading automation, AI support, e-commerce, data, web) and get genuinely good at shipping them.

Every project is built, tested against real data, and deployed by the same person who designed it — so nothing gets lost in translation between "idea" and "running system."
